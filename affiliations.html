Affiliations

Currently I'm the Editor-in-Chief of the Cortex Collective, an experimental multimedia scifi arts collective at Yale, and I work part-time at the Center for Collaborative Arts and Media. I also work with the Yale Grammatical Diversity Project.

In past lives, I cofounded LingLeague and worked as the Editor-in-Chief of Choate Rosemary Hall's oldest literary magazine. I'm also a Bronfman Fellow, an Atlas Finalist, and a member of the Language Creation Society. I've attended SLIYS 2021, CoLang 2024, and spoken at a National Science Foundation conference in 2022.

I'm a current undergraduate student at Yale University, and I studied abroad this summer at John Cabot University in Rome.
