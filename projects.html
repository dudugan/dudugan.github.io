Projects

I've self-published a novella on Amazon, designed and printed a poetry compilation, and written countless short stories. I've also scripted three narrative video games for game jams.

I've composed music for two video games (one above and the other below), and am currently working on a character suite for Fonda Lee's Green Bone Saga, as well as the score of a short sci-fi/western animated film. Using the Tone.js library, I've composed and coded an interactive musical composition from DNA. I also released (to my knowledge) the first ever Old Babylonian rap. I play bass in a band at Yale, and am currently learning songwriting and production skills with the aim to start releasing original work this summer!

I recently coded the website for the fifth volume of the Cortex Collective. I also coded a pathfinding game about indecision, a collection of my friend's laughs, and a website to showcase these projects and more for a class on interactive design and the internet, in addition to the very website you're currently on, all from bare html, css, and javascript! I've also conducted a phylogenetic analysis of all extant Pokémon on the Yale McCleary Computing Cluster and plotted potential evolutionary histories and ancestral trait distributions.

I'm in the process of editing a to-be-published syntactic analysis of the it's giving and just screams constructions for the Yale Grammatical Diversity project, and in high school I conducted a descriptive study on discontinuous phrasal verbs (I turned the lights on vs. I turned on the lights). I'm also a conlanger and youtuber, and I recently wrote a paper on creating n-gram models and recurrent neural networks for data-sparse languages using a conlang of mine.
