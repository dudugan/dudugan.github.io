Projects

I've self-published a novella on Amazon, designed and printed a poetry compilation, and written countless short stories. I've also created two narrative video games for game jams.

In high school I conducted a descriptive study on discontinuous phrasal verbs (I turned the lights on vs. I turned on the lights), and I'm in the process of editing a to-be-published syntactic analysis of the it's giving and just screams constructions for the Yale Grammatical Diversity project. I also recently wrote a paper on creating n-gram models and recurrent neural networks for data-sparse languages (I used a conlang of mine :).

Also, I just released (to my knowledge) the first ever Old Babylonian rap.
