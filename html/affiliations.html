Affiliations
============

Currently I’m the Editor-in-Chief of the Cortex Collective, an experimental multimedia scifi arts collective at Yale, and I work part-time at the Center for Collaborative Arts and Media. I also do research with the Yale Grammatical Diversity Project, and compose for Amoriem Labs, Yale’s game development organization.

In past lives, I cofounded LingLeague and worked as the Editor-in-chief of Choate Rosemary Hall's oldest literary magazine . I’m also a Bronfman Fellow, an Atlas Finalist, and a member of the Language Creation Society. I attended SLIYS 2021, CoLang 2024, and studied abroad in Rome at John Cabot University. I'm a current undergraduate student at Yale University.
